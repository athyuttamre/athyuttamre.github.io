CS Yearbook

Print, Photography · Spring 2015

A recent tradition in the Brown CS department is the CS Yearbook; a fun collection of profiles and photos from the graduating class. With help from a few amazing friends, I designed and photographed for the second volume in 2015.

So long, Class of 2015!
